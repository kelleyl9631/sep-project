Why Join SEP?

SEP is anything but another boring class. If you get bored in the typical classroom setting or if you have a strong urge to find out more about computers and how they work, you should join SEP. Starting from the very first day of class, one can already tell that it is going to be an interesting class. As well as interesting classes, our school is able to give us wonderful opportunities, such as trips to companies and events like Google, AOL, Computer Science Fairs and so much more. With great teachers who are always there to teach you new shortcuts and help you fix your bugs, SEP at HSTAT is definitely one of a kind.
